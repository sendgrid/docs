Access your email statistics via the New Relic Dashboard.

In less than five minutes your stats should be sent to your New Relic Dashboard. We send stats to New Relic every 5 minutes for users with the app enabled.

{% anchor h2 %} Settings {% endanchor %}

If you would like get your statistics in the New Relic Dashboard, you need to provide us your New Relic license key.

This can be found by accessing the SendGrid's Plugin page on New Relic. Or from the Account Settings Page.

In the New Relic app settings you can enable the statistics for your subusers.

{% warning %} You will receive statistics only for 100 subusers {% endwarning %}

After you have entered your settings and saved them, enable the app.

You can setup your app through our API.

{% anchor h2 %} New Relic Dashboard {% endanchor %}

Go on New Relic site and there you will find the SendGrid extension on the left menu. After clicking on 'SendGrid' extension, a SendGrid instance will be displayed and if you activated the subuser options under it will be the stats for subusers.

Click on one of the instances and there you will see the statistics in three categories: Deliveries, Engagement and Compliance. Also on the right side you can see metrics in percentages.
