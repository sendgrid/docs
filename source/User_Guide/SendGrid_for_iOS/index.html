--- layout: page weight: 900 title: SendGrid for iOS Overview seo: title: SendGrid for iOS Overview description: Read about the SendGrid iOS mobile app and how to use it. navigation: show: true --- {% anchor h2 %} What is SendGrid for iOS? {% endanchor %}

SendGrid for your iOS device keeps you connected with your SendGrid account on the go. Keep track of your statistics, manage your suppression lists, view your real-time logs and more.

{% anchor h2 %} System Requirements {% endanchor %}
  • An iOS device with iOS 8 or later
  • A SendGrid account is required. You can sign-up on our website or contact our friendly sales team at 888-985-7363.

SendGrid for your iOS device can be downloaded at the App Store here.
